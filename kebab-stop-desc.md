# Kebab Stop Shop — Landing Page Structure Plan
*Dili, Timor-Leste*

---

## Page Flow Overview

```
Hero → About → Menu → Why Us → Gallery → Reviews → Location → CTA Banner → Footer
```

---

## Section Breakdown

### 1. Hero Section — *First Impression*
**Purpose:** Hook the visitor within the first 3 seconds.

- Full-screen background (hero photo of food or the shop exterior)
- Shop name / logo prominently displayed
- Punchy tagline — e.g. *"Authentic Persian Flavors in the Heart of Dili"*
- Primary CTA button — e.g. **"View Our Menu"** or **"Order Now"**

---

### 2. About / Story Section — *Build Trust*
**Purpose:** Make the brand feel human and authentic.

- Short paragraph about who they are — the origin of recipes, the passion behind the food, the owner's story
- A warm photo of the kitchen, team, or owner
- Establishes credibility and emotional connection with the visitor

---

### 3. Menu / Featured Items Section — *The Core*
**Purpose:** Showcase what they sell and drive appetite.

- Grid or card layout of star dishes (kebabs, stews, rice dishes, sides, drinks)
- Each card includes: food photo, dish name, short description, and price
- Optionally grouped by category (Kebabs / Mains / Sides / Drinks)
- Most-visited section on any food business landing page

---

### 4. Why Us / Highlights Section — *Convince the Undecided*
**Purpose:** Reinforce value and differentiate from competitors.

- 3–4 quick selling points with icons, for example:
  - 🔥 Fresh, daily-grilled kebabs
  - 🌿 Authentic Persian spices imported from source
  - ⚡ Fast service — eat in or takeaway
  - 📍 Centrally located in Dili
- Short, scannable, confidence-building

---

### 5. Gallery Section — *Let the Food Speak*
**Purpose:** Visual proof that the food looks amazing.

- Photo grid or masonry layout
- Mix of close-up food shots and shop atmosphere photos
- People eat with their eyes — this section is especially powerful on mobile
- Links well to Instagram if they have one

---

### 6. Testimonials / Reviews Section — *Social Proof*
**Purpose:** Let happy customers do the selling.

- 2–3 short customer quotes
- Example: *"Best kebab I've had in Timor-Leste!"*
- Can include customer name and a star rating
- Builds trust for first-time visitors

---

### 7. Location & Hours Section — *Drive Foot Traffic*
**Purpose:** Make it easy for people to find and visit the shop.

- Embedded Google Map showing the exact shop location
- Opening hours (days and times clearly listed)
- Phone number and/or WhatsApp link
- Optional: parking notes or nearby landmarks

---

### 8. CTA Banner — *Final Nudge*
**Purpose:** Re-engage visitors who scrolled all the way down.

- Bold full-width strip with a strong line — e.g. *"Hungry? Come Find Us Today"*
- Button or clickable phone number / WhatsApp
- High contrast design to stand out before the footer

---

### 9. Footer — *Wrap Up*
**Purpose:** Provide navigation and contact at a glance.

- Shop logo and short tagline
- Navigation links (jumps to page sections)
- Social media icons — Instagram is essential for food businesses
- Contact info (address, phone, email)
- Copyright line

---

## Notes for Development

- **Mobile-first** — Most visitors in Dili will be on mobile
- **WhatsApp integration** is a must — it's the primary communication channel locally
- **Instagram feed embed** is a nice-to-have if the client is active on it
- **Language** — Consider a Tetum / Portuguese / English toggle if targeting a broader audience
- **Performance** — Compress all food photos for fast load on slower connections
